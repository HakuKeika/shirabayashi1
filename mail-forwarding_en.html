Japan Mail Forwarding Service

Safe receipt and forwarding of mail and packages within Japan for customers residing overseas.

Services > Japan Mail Forwarding Service

Service Features and Mechanism

1

Dedicated Address Provision

We provide a dedicated physical address in Japan for customers to ensure safe and reliable receipt of mail.

2

Efficient Forwarding

We efficiently handle forwarding procedures to anywhere in the world, based on the frequency and method you desire.

3

Strict Confidentiality

We strictly adhere to protecting customer privacy and confidential information, ensuring a trustworthy system.

Flow of Service (STEP BY STEP)

  1. **[Application]**: Please apply for the service using the online form.
  2. **[Address Issuance]**: We will issue and contact you with your exclusive Japanese forwarding address.
  3. **[Mail Receipt]**: We receive and securely store mail/packages addressed to you at our location.
  4. **[Forwarding Instruction]**: You instruct us on the forwarding frequency (weekly, monthly, etc.) and destination.
  5. **[Forwarding & Confirmation]**: Based on your instructions, we package and ship the items, providing tracking information.
