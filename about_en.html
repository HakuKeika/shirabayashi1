Company Overview & Vision

Shirabayashi Corporation: Bridging cultures, building futures since 2012.

Our History and Commitment

Established in **2012**, Shirabayashi Corporation has been dedicated to promoting Japanese culture globally. Simultaneously, we provide essential services to help foreign residents effectively understand and integrate into Japanese culture and society.

Our core mission is to **break down cultural barriers** and provide comprehensive support, ensuring that individuals staying in Japan can lead rich and fulfilling lives, both personally and professionally.

Diverse Business Scope

Our operations span wide-ranging sectors, encompassing international business practices, living integration, and language training programs. We organize conferences related to **overseas investment**, **management consulting**, and **technical advice**.

Our services also include planning and executing **international art exchange projects**, **e-commerce**, **translation services**, **real estate brokerage (buying, selling, leasing, and management)**, **mail collection/forwarding**, and management of **accommodation facilities** (hotels, rentals).

Furthermore, our business extends to **import/export and sales** of environmental goods, electronics, medical devices, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, food, clothing, general merchandise, and liquor. We also handle **used goods sales**, **travel agency services**, **language schools**, **restaurant management**, **transportation**, **nursing care (kaigo)**, **labor dispatch**, **international marriage planning**, **finance**, **beauty salon and seitai (body alignment) clinic operation**, **wood processing**, and **resource waste collection**.

Our Vision

Cultural Integration

To be the primary bridge between Japan and the global community, fostering mutual understanding and cultural exchange through authentic experiences.

Client Partnership

To serve as the most reliable partner for every client, dedicating our comprehensive network and expertise to maximize their potential in Japan.
