Junior High Student Winter Camp (10 Days)

A luxury, comprehensive 10-day cultural immersion and sightseeing tour through the Kansai region.

Services > Cultural Experiences > Student Winter Camp

Duration

10 Days

Location

Kyoto, Osaka, Nara

Group Size

3 to 7 Students (Luxury Small Group)

Pricing & Group Details

Base Fee: ¥14,500 CNY / person

Minimum Group Size: **3 students**. Maximum: **7 students**.

Optional Add-ons (¥1,000 CNY per item/person):

  • Beginner Golf Lesson
  • Skiing/Snowboarding Experience

Fee Includes:

  • All Accommodation (Private Minshuku/Homestay)
  • All Meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • Full-time Private Transportation (Dedicated Vehicle)
  • All Scheduled Sightseeing Entrance Fees

Fee Does NOT Include:

  • Round-trip Airfare to Japan
  • Visa Application Fees
  • Theme Park Tickets (e.g., USJ/Osaka Universal Studios Japan)
  • Travel and Health Insurance (Highly Recommended)
  • Personal Expenses or Unforeseen Costs

Itinerary Highlights

Osaka (The Gourmet Capital)

  • Universal Studios Japan (USJ)
  • Dotonbori (Iconic street food and neon lights)
  • Expo '70 Commemorative Park
  • Umeda Sky Building (Optional)

Kyoto (The Cultural Heart)

  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
  • Kiyomizu-dera (Pure Water Temple)
  • Nijo Castle (Historical Shogunate residence)
  • Kyoto Imperial Palace (Goshō)

Nara (History and Wildlife)

  • Tōdai-ji Temple (Great Buddha Hall)
  • Tōshōdai-ji Temple (Classic Buddhist architecture)
  • Yakushi-ji Temple (Historic complex)
  • Nara Park (Famous deer park)
