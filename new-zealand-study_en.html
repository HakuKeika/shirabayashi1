New Zealand Study & Cultural Exchange

Comprehensive planning and execution for study abroad, cultural exchange, and experience programs in New Zealand.

Services > New Zealand Study & Exchange

Program Highlights and Support

Shirabayashi Corporation offers full-spectrum planning and execution services to ensure a seamless and enriching experience in New Zealand, from academic placement to cultural immersion.

Study Abroad Consulting & Placement

  • Personalized selection of schools and courses based on student goals.
  • Assistance with application, documentation, and visa preparation.
  • Guidance on entrance exams and interviews.

Cultural Exchange & Immersion

  • Planned activities for experiencing Maori culture and traditions.
  • Adventure tourism planning (e.g., hiking, bungee jumping) tailored to safety and interest.
  • Arrangement of local homestays for deep cultural immersion.

Execution and On-site Support

Our team coordinates all logistics, including flight booking support, comprehensive travel insurance advice, and local accommodation arrangements (homestay or student housing). We ensure smooth transitions and offer 24/7 emergency contact support for peace of mind.
